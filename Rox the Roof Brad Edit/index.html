About Rox the Roof

At Rox the Roof, we believe that a strong roof is the foundation of a safe home. Based right here in Lexington, Nebraska, we have been serving our community with integrity and excellence for over 20 years. We understand the unique weather challenges our area faces, from scorching summers to harsh winters, and we build roofs that stand the test of time.

Our team of certified professionals is dedicated to providing top-tier roofing solutions. We treat every home as if it were our own, ensuring meticulous attention to detail and a clean worksite. We are fully licensed, bonded, and insured, giving you peace of mind throughout the entire project.

Whether you need a minor repair, a full replacement, or assistance with an insurance claim, our friendly and knowledgeable staff is here to guide you every step of the way.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of roofing services designed to protect your home and enhance its value. From minor repairs to complete replacements, our experienced team handles every project with precision and care.

Roof Repairs

Don't let small leaks turn into major problems. Our rapid response team provides reliable repairs for leaks, missing shingles, flashing issues, and general wear and tear, extending the life of your roof.

New Roof Installation

Whether you're building a new home or replacing an old roof, we install high-quality roofing systems tailored to your style and budget. We work with asphalt, metal, and tile to give you the perfect look.

Storm Damage Repair

Nebraska weather can be tough. If your roof has been damaged by hail, wind, or severe storms, we offer emergency restoration services and assistance with insurance claims to get your home back to normal fast.

Frequently Asked Questions

Most residential roof replacements are completed within 1-2 days, depending on the size of the roof and weather conditions.

Will you help with insurance claims?

Absolutely. We have extensive experience working with insurance companies and can help guide you through the claims process to ensure you get fair coverage.
