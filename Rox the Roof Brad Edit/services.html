Our Services

Comprehensive Roofing Solutions for Every Need

Expert Roofing Services You Can Trust

At Rox the Roof, we pride ourselves on delivering top-quality workmanship and exceptional customer service. Whether you're dealing with a minor leak or need a complete roof replacement, our team has the expertise and tools to get the job done right. We use only the best materials from trusted manufacturers to ensure your roof stands the test of time.

Roof Repairs

From missing shingles to flashing leaks, we diagnose and fix issues quickly to prevent further damage to your home. Our repair services are thorough and lasting.

Residential Replacement

Upgrade your home's protection and curb appeal with a new roof. We offer a variety of styles and materials, including asphalt shingles, metal, and tile.

Commercial Roofing

We provide durable roofing solutions for businesses of all sizes. Our commercial services include flat roof installation, maintenance, and coatings.

Storm Damage Restoration

When severe weather strikes, we're here to help. We offer emergency tarping, damage assessment, and full restoration services to get your life back to normal.

Roof Inspections

Regular inspections can catch potential problems early. We offer detailed roof inspections for real estate transactions, insurance claims, and routine maintenance.

Gutter Services

Protect your foundation with a properly functioning gutter system. We install, repair, and clean gutters and downspouts to ensure proper water drainage.

Residential & Commercial Expertise

We understand that residential and commercial roofs have different needs. Our team is trained in the specific techniques and materials required for each.

  • Specialized crews for different roof types
  • Minimal disruption to your home or business
  • Safety-first approach on every job site
