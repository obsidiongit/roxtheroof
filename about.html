Our Story

Built Right Here in Lexington

Rox the Roof was founded with a simple mission: to provide exceptional roofing and home improvement services to the Lexington, Nebraska community with honesty, quality, and genuine care for our neighbors.

As a woman-owned and Latin-owned business, we bring a unique perspective to the construction industry. We understand what it means to work hard, build something from nothing, and treat every customer's home as if it were our own.

From our very first roof installation to today, we've grown entirely by earning trust one project at a time. Our customers aren't just clients — they're our neighbors, friends, and community.
