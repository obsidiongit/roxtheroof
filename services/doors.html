Door Wrap

Protect and beautify your home's entry points

Professional Door Wrap Services

Door wrapping is a cost-effective way to protect your entry doors and door frames from the elements while giving them a fresh, updated look. Rox the Roof provides expert door wrap installation using durable aluminum cladding that stands up to Nebraska weather.

Benefits of Door Wrap:

  • Protects door frames from weather damage
  • Eliminates need for regular painting
  • Available in many colors
  • Clean, finished appearance
  • Low maintenance
  • Long-lasting durability
  • Prevents wood rot
  • Increases curb appeal

Why Wrap Your Doors?

Wooden door frames require constant maintenance — painting, sealing, and repairs. Door wrap eliminates this hassle by covering the frame with durable aluminum that never needs painting and resists rust, rot, and weather damage.

We custom-fit each wrap to your door frames for a seamless, professional finish that coordinates with your home's exterior colors.
