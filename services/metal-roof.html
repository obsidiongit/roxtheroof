Metal Roofing

The ultimate in durability and longevity for your property

Built to Last a Lifetime

Metal roofing represents the pinnacle of roofing technology. With lifespans of 50+ years and minimal maintenance requirements, metal roofs offer exceptional value and protection. Rox the Roof is your trusted source for professional metal roof installation in Lexington and surrounding areas.

Benefits of Metal Roofing:

  • 50+ year lifespan
  • Exceptional durability against harsh weather
  • Energy efficient - reflects heat
  • Fire resistant
  • Low maintenance requirements
  • Environmentally friendly - recyclable
  • Increases property value
  • Available in many styles and colors

Perfect for Nebraska Weather

Nebraska's climate can be harsh, with extreme temperatures, high winds, and severe storms. Metal roofing stands up to these challenges better than almost any other material, protecting your home or business year after year.

We offer standing seam, corrugated, and metal shingle options to match your aesthetic preferences and budget.
