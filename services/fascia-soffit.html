Fascia & Soffit

Complete your home's exterior with quality finishing

The Finishing Touch for Your Roofline

Fascia and soffit may not be the most visible parts of your home, but they play a crucial role in protecting your roof and attic from the elements. Rox the Roof provides expert fascia and soffit installation and repair to keep your home protected and looking great.

What Are Fascia & Soffit?

Fascia is the vertical band that runs along the edge of your roof, supporting your gutters and protecting the ends of roof rafters.

Soffit is the underside of your roof overhang, providing ventilation to your attic and preventing moisture damage.

Benefits of Quality Fascia & Soffit:

  • Protects rafters from weather damage
  • Supports gutter systems
  • Provides essential attic ventilation
  • Prevents pest intrusion
  • Enhances curb appeal
  • Available in various colors
  • Low maintenance aluminum options
  • Prevents moisture damage

Signs You Need Fascia/Soffit Repair

Damaged fascia and soffit can lead to serious problems. Look for signs like peeling paint, rot, water stains, pest activity, or visible damage. We can inspect your home and recommend the best course of action.
