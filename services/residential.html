Residential Roofing

Protect your home with expert roofing installation and repairs

Quality Roofing for Your Home

Your home deserves the best protection from the elements. At Rox the Roof, we specialize in residential roofing solutions that combine durability, aesthetics, and lasting value. Whether you need a complete roof replacement or repairs, our experienced team delivers exceptional results.

Our Residential Services Include:

  • Complete roof replacements
  • New construction roofing
  • Roof repairs and patching
  • Asphalt shingle installation
  • Metal roof installation
  • Storm damage repairs
  • Roof inspections
  • Leak detection and repair

Why Choose Us for Your Home?

As a woman-owned, Latin-owned business based right here in Lexington, we understand the importance of your home. We treat every project as if it were our own home, ensuring quality workmanship, honest pricing, and clear communication throughout the process.

We work with top-quality materials from trusted manufacturers and stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties. Your satisfaction is our priority.
