Window Installation

Energy-efficient windows that enhance your home

Beautiful, Efficient Windows

New windows can transform your home's appearance while dramatically improving energy efficiency. Rox the Roof provides professional window installation services, helping you upgrade to modern, high-performance windows that save money and add value to your home.

Benefits of New Windows:

  • Lower energy bills
  • Better temperature control
  • Reduced outside noise
  • Enhanced curb appeal
  • Increased home value
  • Improved security features
  • Easier cleaning and maintenance
  • UV protection for furnishings

Signs It's Time for New Windows

If you're experiencing drafts, condensation between panes, difficulty opening/closing windows, or high energy bills, it may be time for window replacement. We can assess your current windows and recommend the best options for your home and budget.

From double-hung to casement, bay to picture windows, we install a variety of styles to match your home's architecture and your personal preferences.
